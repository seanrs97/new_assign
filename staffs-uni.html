What's Here

Take a look at what Staffs has to offer!

This list has been created to guide you through your physical life at Staffs Uni

Main Facilities on Campus

  • Thompson Library

    The only library on the Stoke-on-Trent campus and the main source for students to study through the use of books and computers that the library provides. Located at the rear end of the campus, near the train station and the Mellor building.

  • Mellor Building

    The main building for It and any computing and engineering students. Located right in the heart of the rear campus.

  • Brindley Building

    Main building for business students, also includes a food court that is available for all students and staff. Located in the heart of the main campus, near the entrance.

  • Science Centre

    Staffs uni's main building and the heart of most events and of course Science! Located on Leek road, you can't miss it really, it's the big futuristic building next to Stoke 6th Form.

  • Law Building

    The main building for all Law students. Located on the main campus, near the Astro turf and main fields.

  • Flaxman Building

    The main building for art students and also home to the University theatre. Located on the rear campus, opposite the Thompson Library and the Mellor building.

  • Sir Stanley Matthews sports centre

    The main building for all indoor sports and home to the Staffs University gym. Located in the hear of the main campus, near the LRV and halls of residence.

Main points in and around Staffordshire University

  • Intu Potteries

    The main shopping, entertainment and food centre of stoke, here you'll find all of your large retailers and restaurants such as Primark, River Island, Nando's and Cineworld. Located in the center of Hanley town.

  • The Ember Lounge

    The local Uni bar/pub and the place to go for a quiet drink or as a start to your night on the weekend! Ember lounge is located on site, in the centre of the rear campus.

  • LRV

    The only Uni club and the most popular one amongst students. LRV is located on site, in the center of the main campus.

  • The Sugarmill

    The sugarmill is the best place in stoke in terms of live music. It draws in some pretty large rock, metal and indie bands on a weekly basis, aswell as providing weekly club nights! It is located on the edge of hanley, on the club strip.

  • Fiction

    The bigges club in Hanley and the go to spot for students on a night out! Located on the edge of hanley, on the club strip.

  • Trentham Estate

    Trentham is a large estate of gardens, forests and even a shopping village that is a nice breath of fresh air from the busy city life. Trentham is located on the outskirts of Stoke, towards Stone.

  • Hanley Park

    Hanley park is one of the largest parks in Stoke and is also one of the nicest and busiest, when it's a nice day. It is located just outside of campus, in the middle of both campuses, towards hanley.
